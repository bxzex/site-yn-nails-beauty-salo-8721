Got Questions?

Frequently Asked.

Do I need to make an appointment?

While we do accept walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to secure your preferred time and technician.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, and cash.

Are your tools sanitized?

Absolutely. Cleanliness is our top priority. All metal tools are sterilized in a medical-grade autoclave, and we use disposable liners for our pedicure bowls.

Do you offer group bookings or bridal parties?

Yes! We love hosting bridal parties, birthdays, and group events. Please call us directly to arrange a private group booking.
